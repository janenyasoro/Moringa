Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Fire Island?

Fire Island is accessible by ferry from several Long Island towns including Bay Shore, Sayville, and Patchogue. There are no bridges connecting to the island.

When is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit is between May and September when the weather is warm and all facilities are open. Summer offers the best beach weather.

Are there accommodations on the island?

Yes, there are hotels, bed & breakfasts, and rental homes available in various communities throughout the island.

Is Fire Island pet-friendly?

Pets are allowed on beaches and trails but must be leashed. Some areas have seasonal restrictions to protect wildlife.
